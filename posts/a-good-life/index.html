A Good Life

Table of Contents


Draft - This post is a work-in-progress.

My wife's friend died recently and we just attended her funeral. I was struck by her rich circle of friends and family. She was close to many loving, wonderful people. She died from a sudden heart attack but not long before her death she told a friend that was happier than ever.

She lived a good life.

I've been kind of obsessed with figuring out a recipe for a good life lately. Maybe that's becaue I turned 40 recently and all of a sudden time feels finite, so I want to make the best of it.

I haven't been looking at this question from a mindset or values perspective. I'm not thinking about things like accepting yourself or appreciating what you have. Instead, I'm asking "how should I spend my time?".

Evereyone's different but here's my recipe so far:

Connecting with others

Connecting with friends and family is key to living a good life. This could be:
  • Dinner with your family
  • Playing a game with friends
  • Having a deep conversation with a loved one
Spend quality time with people you love. The more, the better.

Playing

You're playing when you do fun things that you enjoy. Think of things like:
  • Skiing
  • Reading a good book
  • Hiking
  • Dancing with friends (bonus points for connecting with others)
  • Playing guitar
  • Gardening
  • Playing sports
  • Playing video games

Any leisure activity is play.

As we get older we get serious and too busy to play. But it's what makes life interesting.

Working

Do good work that you're proud of. Big bonus points if your work is meaningful to you.

Work isn't always paid. For example, if you're helping a friend build a deck, you're working. A life with a lot of work is a good life. We feel better when we're productive. The point is to care about your work and be proud of whatever you accomplish.

Personal Growth

When we're working toward a goal and improving ourselves, we feel good.

Life is good when we're working on ourselves.

This could mean learning at school, practicing a skill, training for a competition.

Helping Others

Truly helping others is the highest level of being.

If your friend finds themself in the hospital and needs you to do walk their dog.

For me this is means teaching coding to youngsters.

Staying Healthy

Everything is better when your mind and body are healthy. When that's not the case, we suffer. It takes a lot of time and hard work to maintain good health, but it's essential.
