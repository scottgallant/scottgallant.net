How to build a free blog that you own and control.

Why "Bulletproof"?

Because it's built with HTML and CSS. No JavaScript. No third-party services. No ads. No tracking. No bullshit.

  • Free
  • 100% control. Not locked into any service.

Who is this for?

  • People who are not scared of learning new things (HTML)
  • People who are excited about extending and creating
